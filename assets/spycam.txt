*SpyCam is all about recording videos without any preview.

*Since there is no preview available app works in hidden mode.

*SpyCam let you record videos in background.

*You can record a video even if your phone screen is locked.

             ********Works In Two Mode*********** 


*Instant Recording :- Starts Recording immediately And Pushes the app in background.(Can Start Instant Mode by clicking on SpyCam in Menu)
*Timer Recording :- Starts Recording on set time.(Can Start By clicking on timer Recorder in menu)
